## Overview

**From Horsepower to Headsets** explores 120 years of British employment transformation through census data (1901â€“2021).  
It traces the decline of horse and coal industries, the rise of the automotive era, and the shift to service-sector work, particularly call centres.  

---

## Key Insights

- **1901**: 2.3% employed in horse-related work; 2.4% in coal  
- **1971**: Car trade employs 875,000; horses nearly vanish from the economy  
- **2021**: 7.5% of workforce in customer service & call centres
